# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful and productive experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying specific about technical concerns.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Avoid sharing private prompts, secrets, local paths, or other sensitive transcript content.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Repeated off-topic or disruptive comments.
- Sharing exploit details publicly before maintainers have a chance to respond.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation for behavior that violates this code of conduct.
